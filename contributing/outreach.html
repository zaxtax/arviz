Outreach¶

Caution

Page in construction

You can also contribute to ArviZ by doing outreach, writing blogposts, getting people to use it at your company or university, helping external libraries integrate with ArviZ (in any programming language of your choice).

If you have a website or blog where you often post about ArviZ, a library that integrates with ArviZ reach out to us or open a PR to add your blog to Community, we’d love to hear from you!
